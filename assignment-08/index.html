HOW TO UPLOAD A VIDEO TO YOUTUBE!



How it all works:

YouTube has made it really easy for people of all skill levels to publish their own content. That being said, some people still find it difficult when it come to actually posting new videos to the site.

Step by Step:

  1. Step 1: Look for the icons in the upper right of the screen.


  2. Step 2: Click the Camera icon.


  3. Step 3: Source your files from your computer.


  4. Step 4: Click "upload".


  5. Step 5: Wait for your video to finish uploading and processing. This is where you could make the video private or public as well as choose a thumbnail.


  6. Step 6: Click "Publish" and you're done! Congratulations! Make sure your video is 100% done processing!

    7. Look at that! You've uploaded a video to YouTube!
