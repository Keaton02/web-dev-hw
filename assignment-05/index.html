French Toast for One


Ingredients

  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 0.25 cup of milk, light cream, or half-and-half
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon margarine or butter
  • Maple-flavored syrup
  • Fresh fruit (optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1: In a shallow flat dish beat together egg and milk with a fork until combined. Dip bread slices in egg mixture. In a medium skillet melt margarine over medium-heat.
  2. Step 2: Cook dipped bread slices in margarine for 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Serve with maple-flavored syrup. Garnish with fresh fruit.
